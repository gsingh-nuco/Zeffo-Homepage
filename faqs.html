A Zeffo gift card lets you gift mutual fund units to someone. The recipient can redeem it and the units are held in their name at the fund house.

Choose an AMC, select the amount, personalise your message, pay online and the gift card is delivered to the recipient via email or SMS.

We currently support ICICI Prudential, HDFC, and Motilal Oswal mutual funds, with more AMCs being added soon.

Yes, you will receive a confirmation with details. You can also contact us at support@zeffo.com for any queries about your purchase.

The recipient receives a unique code. They complete a simple KYC process and the mutual fund units are allocated in their name.

Gift cards have a redemption window. However, once redeemed, the mutual fund units have no expiry and stay invested until the holder decides to withdraw.

Once redeemed, your units are held at the fund house. You can import and view them in most popular investing apps like Groww, Kuvera, or MFCentral.
