From the founder

I've spent the last decade building financial products. My training at MIT and Harvard Law School and years of practicing law have shaped how I think about innovation and trust.

My roots are in India. My mom was from Mumbai and my dad from Varanasi. As a teen, I spent months at a time traveling by local train, playing carrom with cousins, and learning how seriously families value money.

Indian families take pride in planning carefully, avoiding waste, and thinking long-term about their children's futures. My own family is the same, and Zeffo is built to honor that foundation.

This isn't a side project for me. Building something worthy of our customers' trust motivates me every day. It's a responsibility I take seriously.

Avi Lele
Founder, Zeffo
